# **App Name**: Totekafe Online

## Core Features:

- Product Display: Showcase Totekafe's coffee products with images, descriptions, and prices.
- Shopping Cart: Enable customers to add products to a cart and manage their selections.
- Secure Checkout: Provide a secure checkout process with payment gateway integration.
- Order Management: Allow administrators to manage orders, track shipments, and update order statuses.
- AI-Powered Product Recommendations: Suggest coffee products to users based on their browsing history using an AI tool.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep brown (#6F4E37) evoking the rich color of coffee.
- Background color: Light tan (#F5E7D3), a desaturated tint of the primary, creates a warm and inviting feel.
- Accent color: Gold (#B8860B), an analogous hue, signifies premium quality and adds a touch of luxury.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for headlines, suggesting an elegant and fashionable brand. Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for body, chosen for its modern look and readability.
- Use coffee-related icons for navigation and category representation.
- Clean and simple layout with high-quality product photography.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience.
